Team Pulse v0.15.3 release notes

Summary
- Fixed save-state feedback so manual saves show the saved time in the header pill.
- Shortened transient notification banners from about 4.2 seconds to 2 seconds.

What changed
- Clicking Save now leaves the header save-state pill in a manual-save state such as Saved 11:49 instead of Autosaved.
- Autosave remains the primary persistence path and still flushes after a short debounce of about 160 ms once a data-changing action has been committed.
- All transient toast/banner notifications now dismiss after 2000 ms.

Compatibility
- No schema or file-format changes in this patch release.
- Existing Team Pulse folders and JSON files remain compatible.
